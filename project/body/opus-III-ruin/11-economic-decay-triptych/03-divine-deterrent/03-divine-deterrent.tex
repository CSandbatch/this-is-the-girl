\PoemHead{Divine Deterrent}{– for a girl spinning in broadcast snow}

\begin{stanza}
God won’t foreclose if breasts are bare\\
    and angels orbit ICBMs.\\
    Texas girls know what’s not there!\\
God won’t foreclose if breasts are bare.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
She mouths the pledge with vacant stare.\\
    Her silence tallies market whims,\\
    and angels orbit ICBMs.\\
God won’t foreclose if breasts are bare.
\end{stanza}
