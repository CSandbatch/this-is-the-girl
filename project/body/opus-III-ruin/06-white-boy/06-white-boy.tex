\PoemHead{White Boy}{}

\begin{stanza}
María\\
\quad who waits for you at home waits for very long\\
\quad damn how little we understand\\
\quad when we ride on your side of the sun.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Trying to dream in \textit{rayo, loco de tanto sol}\\
\quad \textit{Cuando irrumpimos en el rostro del juicio.}\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
This room gets hot early \& faces the sun.\\
No one called all yesterday.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Finger to the window—morning creep—oil rainbow;\\
\quad dogs chase clouds down the street.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Luck’s a witch, not a hare.\\
Door’s open sun’s up nobody watching.\\
Two clean shirts left. Wear a dirty one.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
So if there’s any softness in you?\\
Some turn of grace?\\
Some sly smile for me—give it now.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
\quad Thin burn all the veils.
\end{stanza}
