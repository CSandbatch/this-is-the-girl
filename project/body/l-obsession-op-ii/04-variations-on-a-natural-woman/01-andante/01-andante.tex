\begin{stanza}
Some lace on the sill, and the musk of old rooms,\\
A spring twig of longing under lemon-tree blooms.\\
It is nothing, you say, just a street in the sun,\\
But your calves in the daylight, the way that you run\\
I will think of the salt on your skin and your care.\\
