\PoemHead{Graveyard Lesson}{}

\begin{stanza}
This world—\\
\quad shelter for the stranger,\\
\quad not for the dweller.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Other days:\\
\quad hope,\\
\quad vigil,\\
\quad arrival.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Oh soul—\\
\quad how long in sleep?\\
\quad how long numb to the wind of your fate?\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
Listen:\\
\quad to those who have walked far,\\
\quad to the old who know the crossways.\\
\end{stanza}

\begin{stanza}
What I have gathered—\\
\quad years of knowledge, years of sorrow;\\
\quad before their stony sepulchres,\\
\qquad reading silent lessons\\
\qquad of those gone before.
\end{stanza}
