\PoemHead{Night}{}

\begin{stanza}
My voice for you; so gentle, so exhausted\\
Bestirs nocturnal silence, deep and frosted.\\
Beside my bed a melancholy flame\\
Burns on; my verses merge, descend, the same\\
As little streams of love, all fed by you.\\
Within the dark your eyes still strike me through,\\
They shine, they lean toward mine; I hear your breath\\
My friend, tenderest my friend\ldots\ I love you\ldots
\end{stanza}

\vspace{0.5\baselineskip}

\begin{stanza}
\raggedleft
Odessa\\
26 April, 1823\\
--- Alexander Pushkin
\end{stanza}
